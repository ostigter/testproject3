Texas Hold'em Poker
===================

Phases of each hand:
 1. Rotate the dealer position.
 2. Post the small and big blinds.
 3. Deal the 2 Hole cards per player.
 4. Pre-Flop betting round.
 5. Deal the 3 Flop cards on the board.
 6. Flop betting round.
 7. Deal the Turn card on the board.
 8. Turn betting round.
 9. Deal the River card on the board.
10. River betting round.
11. Showdown.

Hole cards, raised:          Call, Raise or Fold

Flop/Turn/River, first position:            Check, Bet or Fold
Flop/Turn/River, other positions, checked:  Check, Bet or Fold
Flop/Turn/River, other positions, bet:      Call, Raise or Fold
Flop/Turn/River, other positions, raised:   Call, Raise or Fold

Check: (noOfBoardCards > 0) && (bet == 0)

Player action algorithm
-----------------------

Factors:
- hole cards
- board cards (Flop, Turn, River)
- number of players
- table position
- cash
- bet
- pot
- bluffing (self)
- actions opponents earlier positions
- opponent history
- opponent aggression
- bluffing opponents
